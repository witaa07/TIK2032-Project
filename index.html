What it is Multimedia?

Multimedia is the combination of various media such as text, images, sound, video, animation, and interaction used together in one presentation or application. The main purpose of multimedia is to convey information to users in a more engaging, interactive, and effective manner.

What it is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a decentralized network of interconnected blocks containing validated transactions, cryptographically linked together. It ensures transparency, security, and decentralization. Widely used in finance, logistics, and healthcare for its innovative applications like cryptocurrencies and supply chain management.
